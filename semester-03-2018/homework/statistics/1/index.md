        $$k_1=2, n_1=5, k_2=3, n_2=8$$
        $$6720- (\frac{5!}{3!}\cdot \frac{1}{5}\cdot \frac{8!}{5!}\cdot \frac{1}{8})$$
        $$6720- (4 \cdot 6 \cdot 7)$$
        $$6720-168$$
        $$6552$$
